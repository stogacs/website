Widener Preliminary Contest

Welcome to the Widener Preliminary Contest! Here, you'll compete against your fellow CS-ians and try to represent Conestoga at the Widener Competition on Thursday, Nov 16, 2022

  • You'll have 6 problems to do within 30 minutes
  • Each problem has a 2 second runtime limit (but no memory limit)
  • Each problem has 10 test cases - you will only be told whether they passed (correct output in time limit) or not
  • Your score will be a cumulative of time spent to solve and test cases correct
