About Us

Hello World, and welcome to the Conestoga Computer Science Club website! Since 2012, we've empowered students of all backgrounds to explore computer science through hands-on activities, tech talks, and events. In addition to our weekly meetings (on Mondays in room 2005), we organize a variety of events throughout the year. Our flagship event is hackStoga (Stoga's annual hackathon), but we also organize a Scratchathon, two CodeFests, and participate and sometimes win local events. You should join our Schoology group!
