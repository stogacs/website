Our Mission

Inspiring students to explore the world of computer science outside of the classroom by providing hands-on experience with coding and teamwork, and offering a diverse and inclusive environment where students of all backgrounds can learn and grow together.

What hackStoga Looks Like

For the past four years, we've been hosting hackStoga, and it's been drawing hundreds of students from local school districts! With skill levels ranging from beginner to expert, club-sponsored workshops, an engaging estimathon by Jane Street, and pizza and merch, it's always a blast! This year, we're planning on scaling it up and expanding our workshops to reach a broader audience and foster a more inclusive high school programming culture.

Why Support Us

Build brand awareness and reach motivated students from across Southeastern PA.

Inspire future talent and help students discover computer science and develop essential skills.

Connect with talented students interested in technology careers and internship opportunities.

How You Can Help

Sponsor hackStoga through direct donations to support the event and help cover essential event costs.

Contribute food, prizes, or merchandise to enhance the experience and showcase your brand to attendees.

Have a unique idea of specialty? Please reach out to us! We're always looking for new ways to engage our attendees.

Sponsorship Tiers

Bronze

With the Bronze tier, your brand gets the initial level of exposure at a starter package price. The benefits you get include:

  • 2 hours of booth time
  • Logo shared on website and atrium screens
  • Small logo on hackStoga shirts
  • Mention during opening and closing ceremonies.
  • Thank-you shout-out on social media and pre-event emails

Silver

$500

At the Silver tier, you get added visibility with the potential for greater brand engagement. You'll also receive:

  • 4 hours of booth time
  • 30 minutes of classroom or workshop time
  • Dedicated sponsor feature on social media and pre-event emails
  • Medium logo on hackStoga shirts

Includes all benefits from the Bronze tier.
