Our Mission

Inspiring students to explore the world of computer science outside of the classroom by providing hands-on experience with coding and teamwork, and offering a diverse and inclusive environment where students of all backgrounds can learn and grow together.

What hackStoga Looks Like

For the past four years, we've been hosting hackStoga, and it's been drawing hundreds of students from local school districts! With skill levels ranging from beginner to expert, club-sponsored workshops, an engaging estimathon by Jane Street, and pizza and merch, it's always a blast! This year, we're planning on scaling it up and expanding our workshops to reach a broader audience and foster a more inclusive high school programming culture.
