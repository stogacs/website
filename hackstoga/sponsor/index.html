Our Mission

Inspiring students to explore the world of computer science outside of the classroom by providing hands-on experience with coding and teamwork, and offering a diverse and inclusive environment where students of all backgrounds can learn and grow together.

What A hackStoga Looks Like


For the past two years, we hosted hackStoga, drawing hundreds students from many local school districts. We received numerous project submissions from beginners to experts, with club-sponsored coding workshops and an engaging 3D printing seminar by the Uncommon Individual Foundation. This year, we're expanding workshops to reach a broader audience and foster our local high school programming culture.

Sponsorship Tiers

Bronze

$100-250

With the bronze tier, your brand will be featured prominently on hackStoga prompotional material, giving you visibility and exposure to a highly engaged audience. You'll also receive:

  • Your logo on our hackStoga T-Shirt (If you confirm a month before hackStoga)
  • Your logo projected to the atrium's two large screens

Silver

$250-500

In the silver tier, you'll have the opportunity to showcase your products and services, engage with attendees, and build relationships with potential customers. You'll also receive:

Gold

$500-1500

In the gold tier, you'll be recognized as a leader and supporter of hackStoga. You'll also receive:

  • All-day booth time
