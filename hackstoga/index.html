What is a Hackathon?

Good question! A Hackathon is an event where students come together to learn, build, and share their creations the course of the day. Hackathons are often paired with delicious (and free!) food and shiny prizes.

Anybody with an interest in technology can attend and expect a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. You certainly don't have to be a programmer or even know how to code.

About hackStoga

Join over a hundred hackers from around the tri-state area in attending hackStoga on March 25, 2023!

Whether you're a seasoned hacker or you're new to tech, hackStoga is open to everyone! Create a project, learn new skills, attend workshops, and connect with friends at the event. (Did we mention? There's gonna be free food!)

Our Faq

General

Where and when is the event?

hackStoga will take place Saturday March 25th, 2022 at Conestoga High School. Currently, we plan on holding it in Conestoga's Atrium.

Do I have to bring anything?

Well, a laptop! We recommend that you bring a charger as well and your phone (especially if it has a hotspot). As for non-tangible items: any libraries you may need (in case there are technical hiccups) and an enthusiasm to program!

How much does hackStoga cost?

hackStoga is 100% free for all participants thanks to our wonderful sponsors!

Hey, I don't have any coding experience!

Of course! In addition to mentors to help you we'll have introductory programming classes, opportunities for artists, a Smash Bros tournament (although that works for anyone), and more.

I'm a middle schooler/college student, can I come?

Yep!

Teams & Projects

Do I need a team?

While we recommend having a teams of 2-5, you can also particpate solo as well.

Will there be a theme?

There is, and it will be announced at hackStoga.

When can I start working on my project?

You get to start on your project when hackStoga starts, after the morning presentation.

Can I work on previous projects?

No, all projects must be built from scratch and started after our morning presentation. Although, you are welcome to brainstorm ideas ahead of time.
