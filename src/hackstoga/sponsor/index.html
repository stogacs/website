HackStoga

HackStoga is an all-day hackathon hosted by the Conestoga High School Computer Science Club. Bringing in students from all across the tri-state area, we hope to allow students of all status and ability to turn their visions into reality through their project forged with teamwork, imagination, and skill. Our goal is to spark a passion for computer science in our hackers so that they may continue to explore the vast world of coding outside our walls.

Coming March 25, 2023 — Conestoga High School

Our Social Mission Of Impact

#1 To foster a passion for computer science in our hackers through an applied-concept programming competition

#2 To infuse a computer science experience with equity

What A HackStoga Looks Like

Last year, we hosted the first annual HackStoga. Attracting over a hundred students from 5 school districts, HackStoga certified itself as a successful community event. HackStoga 2022 saw dozens of project submissions from hardworking teams consisting of novice beginners to experienced masters. Club-sponsored Intro-to-Code workshops held at the event boosted beginners' programming abilities. Additionally, Uncommon Individual Foundation provided an informative and well-received seminar on 3D printing that attracted a variety of interested students. We plan to expand our workshops even further this year, attracting an even larger audience to further our mission to cultivate a local, high school programming culture.
