HackStoga

HackStoga is the annual hackathon hosted by Conestoga High School's Computer Science Club. This event provides a unique opportunity for companies to connect with aspiring tech talent from across the tri-state area. By sponsoring HackStoga, companies can support and inspire a new generation of coders, and gain valuable access to a highly engaged and enthusiastic talent-pool. Sponsoring HackStoga is a great way to show your commitment to fostering the growth of the tech industry.

Our Mission

Inspiring students to explore the world of computer science outside of the classroom by providing hands-on experience with coding and teamwork, and offering a diverse and inclusive environment where students of all backgrounds can learn and grow together.

What A HackStoga Looks Like

Last year, we hosted the first annual HackStoga. Attracting over a hundred students from 5 school districts, HackStoga has established itself as a successful community event. HackStoga 2022 saw dozens of project submissions from hardworking teams consisting of novice beginners to experienced masters. Club-sponsored Intro-to-Code workshops held at the event boosted beginners' programming abilities. Additionally, the Uncommon Individual Foundation provided an informative and well-received seminar on 3D printing that attracted a variety of interested students. We plan to expand our workshops even further this year, attracting an even larger audience to further our mission to cultivate a local, high school programming culture.
